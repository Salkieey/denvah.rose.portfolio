Isometric Room: Toymaker's Studio

Project Outline

In my studies I was tasked with making mutliple assets each week, my last task was to put these assets together to make an environmental scene. The two assets I chose were my toy train and cat robot.

My Process

While brainstorming ideas for environment's, I found various images of isometric rooms, as this was one of my bigger projects, I wanted to create something visually pleasing, but simple so I could finish it before the deadline. I beleived a isometric room would be a great way to execute a simple scene, that can still be detailed.

I also settled on the idea for a toymakers studio, because I modeled these two assets after children's toys. I thought having a toy story inspired scene would also bring more character to a simple environment, and aligned with the theme.
